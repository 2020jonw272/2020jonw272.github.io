A Lapse of Sanity

You attempt to speak only to be found by an awkward silence that continues to purvey the room. You thought if the idea to write what you wanted to say, but with your arms bound and its apparent lack of sensory organs this would likely be impossible. The being whose skin appears to be thick like bark extends a finger towards you slowly. It presses on the base your neck which you are unable to see from your current position and you find yourself able to speak. In a garbled scratchy voice you say “Wh..Who am I?” The being responds almost immediately “Sie stellen mir diese Frage jeden Tag und ich gebe Ihnen jeden Tag die gleiche Antwort. Genauso wie wenn Sie in dem Würfel leben würden, den ich in der Hand halte, sehen Sie jeden Tag als eine neue Erfahrung im Unendlichen.” You stood baffled as once more you understood nothing.
